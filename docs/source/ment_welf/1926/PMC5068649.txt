Dr. Robert Henry Cole
======================

Mental Welfare. 97
We regret to have to record the death of Dr. Robert Henry Cole, Physician
for Mental Diseases at St. Mary's Hospital, Examiner in Mental Diseases and
Psychology, University of London, and last year's President of the Section of
Psychiatry of the Royal Society of Medicine. He had for many years represented
the Medico-Psychological Association on the Executive Council of the C.A.M.W.
