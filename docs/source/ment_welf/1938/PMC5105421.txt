Keep Fit Singing Games
========================

With an Intro-
duction on How to Treat Typical Faults
in Posture. By Gertrude L. Salinger.
Evans Brothers Ltd. 2/6.
This is an attractive book, gaily bound and
clearly printed, with numerous explanatory
illustrations.
It consists of a series of songs accom-
panied by movements which are simple
physical exercises in the form of games.
We are told in the preface that the games
are the " result of long study in the leading
gymnastic methods, in remedial work, and
in child psychologyThe tunes are
strongly rhythmic.
Designed for young normal children, the
hook should be of help to those who are
training low-grade defectives in institutions
and Occupation Centres.
