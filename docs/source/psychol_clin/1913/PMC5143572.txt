Fourth International Congress on School Hygiene.
The Fourth International Congress on School Hygiene will be held
in Buffalo, New York, August 25 to 30, 1913. The three preceding
congresses were held in Nuremberg (1904), London (1907) and Paris
(1910). Readers of The Psychological Clinic desiring full informa-
tion about the congress, its organization, program, etc., may address
the Secretary-General, Dr. Thomas A. Storey, College of the City of
New York, New York, N. Y.
